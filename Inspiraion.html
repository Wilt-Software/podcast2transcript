Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Email us at [email protected].

1. How much does it cost?
USD $0.06 per minute + $1 per file. For example, a 30-minute audio would cost USD $2.80 = $0.06 * 30 + $1. No subscription is needed. It's pay-as-you-go.
2. How long does it take?
It takes less than 1 minute to transcribe a 60-minute audio. Longer audio files may take longer and shorter audio files may take less time. We'll email you once it's done. Or you can check the status on the dashboard.
3. What audio/video formats do you support?
We support common audio and video file formats, including .aac, .flac, .flv, .m4a, .m4b, .m4p, .m4r, .mp3, .mpga, .ogg, .oga, .mogg, .wav, .wma, .webm, .mov, .mp4, .m4v...

Pro-tip: Find podcast audio easily at ListenNotes.com.

4. What languages do you support?
We support English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, and Dutch. We can automatically detect the language in the audio file. Let us know if you need other languages: [email protected]
5. How does the transcript look like?
The transcript is in multiple file formats to fit your need, including plain texts, srt, vtt, and json.
